flex: initial
equivalnet to flex: 0 1 auto 

Sizes items based height and width. Does not grow, but does shrink. 

flex: auto 
equivalent to flex: 1 1 auto 
makes container both grow and shrink 

flex: none
flex: 0 0 auto 
makes item not flexible, and sets them according to width and height properties 

flex: <positive number>
flex: <positive number> 1 0
makes item flexible and sets thr flex basis to zero, resulting in an item that receives the specified proportion of free space
